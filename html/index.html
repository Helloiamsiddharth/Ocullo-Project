Places & Books

OCULLO Foundation is a not for profit/social enterprise run by a team of professionals and volunteers who provide educational programs and information to schools, community and individuals to promise awareness and informed personal actions concerning environmental and health issues.

Ocullo Foundation seeks to connect youth and the community with nature and the natural environment.
